What is Solve et Coagula?

Solve et Coagula is a simple web application that allows you to create and manage your own alchemical recipes.

How does it work?

First, you need to create an account. Then, you can create your own recipes, or use the ones created by other users.

What is alchemy?

Alchemy is an ancient practice that combines elements of chemistry, physics, astrology, art, semiotics, metallurgy, medicine, mysticism, and religion. It is considered a protoscience that contributed to the development of modern chemistry and medicine.

What is the goal of alchemy?

The goal of alchemy is to transform base metals into noble metals, such as gold. Alchemists also sought to create the elixir of life, a potion that would grant eternal life.
